Compliance Frameworks We Support

Comprehensive coverage of major regulatory and industry standards

GDPR

General Data Protection Regulation compliance for organizations handling EU personal data.

  • • Data mapping & inventory
  • • Privacy impact assessments
  • • Data subject rights implementation
  • • Breach notification procedures

HIPAA

Healthcare compliance for protecting patient health information and medical records.

  • • Risk assessments
  • • Administrative safeguards
  • • Physical & technical safeguards
  • • Business associate agreements

SOX

Sarbanes-Oxley Act compliance for financial reporting and internal controls.

  • • Internal control assessments
  • • Financial reporting controls
  • • IT general controls (ITGC)
  • • Audit documentation

ISO 27001

International standard for information security management systems (ISMS).

  • • ISMS implementation
  • • Risk assessment methodology
  • • Security control selection
  • • Certification preparation

PCI DSS

Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard for organizations handling card data.

  • • Cardholder data environment
  • • Network security controls
  • • Access control measures
  • • Regular security testing

SOC 2

Service Organization Control 2 for service providers handling customer data.

  • • Trust services criteria
  • • Security & availability
  • • Processing integrity
  • • Confidentiality & privacy
